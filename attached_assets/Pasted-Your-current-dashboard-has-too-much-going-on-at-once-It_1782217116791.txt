### My recommendation

For a badminton club app, the dashboard should really be:

**Hero**
↓
**Next Session**
↓
**Quick Actions**
↓
**Performance**
↓
**Announcements**
↓
**Community**

Everything else belongs inside its own page. The moment you remove 60-70% of those widgets, the platform will instantly feel more premium.
