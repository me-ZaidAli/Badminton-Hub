Match Momentum Graph (The Killer Feature)

This graph shows how the rivalry evolved over time, not just who won.

Instead of just showing results, it visually answers:

Who dominated the rivalry

When momentum changed

Whether the rivalry is close or one-sided

📈 How It Works

Each match updates a cumulative score line.

Example rivalry:

Match	Winner	Rivalry Score
1	Tarik	0–1
2	Tarik	0–2
3	Haider	1–2

Graph output:

Tarik
 2 |        ●
 1 |    ●
 0 | ●
   +----------------
     M1  M2  M3

If Haider starts winning later, the line moves back up.

This instantly shows momentum shifts.
