Live group tables

Knockout

Bracket generation

Auto-advance winners

Support walkovers and byes

Group Stage + Knockout

Group qualification rules (e.g. top 2)

Auto-populate knockout stage

League / Swiss

Multiple rounds

Ranking recalculation after each round
