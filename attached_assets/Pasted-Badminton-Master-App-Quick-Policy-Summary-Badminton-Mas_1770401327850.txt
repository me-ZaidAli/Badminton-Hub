Badminton Master App – Quick Policy Summary

Badminton Master is operated by Dragon Badminton Club – BPG Ltd.
The app is used to book badminton sessions, manage attendance, process payments, and communicate club information.

By using this app, you agree to:

Provide accurate registration details

Book and attend sessions responsibly

Follow club rules and coach instructions

Accept that badminton activities carry some risk

Your personal data is used only to run the club, manage sessions, and communicate with you. We do not sell your data.

Full Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions are available within the app.

3. Privacy Policy (Plain English, In-App)
Privacy Policy – Badminton Master App

Who we are
Badminton Master is operated by Dragon Badminton Club – BPG Ltd, a UK registered company.

What data we collect

Name and contact details

Date of birth (for age grouping and safeguarding)

Emergency contact details

Session bookings and attendance

Payment and transaction records

App usage information

Why we need it
We use your data to:

Book and manage badminton sessions

Communicate updates, changes, and announcements

Manage payments and attendance

Meet safeguarding and legal requirements

Improve how the app works

Who can see your data

Club administrators and authorised coaches

Trusted service providers (e.g. payment processors)
We do not sell or misuse your personal data.

How long we keep it
We keep your data only as long as needed for club, legal, or safeguarding purposes.

Your rights
You can:

Ask to see your data

Ask for corrections

Ask for deletion where legally possible

Withdraw consent for non-essential communication

Requests should be made to Dragon Badminton Club – BPG Ltd.
