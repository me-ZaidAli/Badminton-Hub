🔹 1. STANDARD WEEKLY HOURS — 1 CLICK FOR ENTIRE WEEK 🚀
*FASTEST OPTION — set your usual working hours for all days at once*
✅ New Section: **"⚡ SET STANDARD WEEK HOURS"**
   • Inputs:
     - Working Days: [✅ Mon] [✅ Tue] [✅ Wed] [✅ Thu] [✅ Fri] [❌ Sat] [❌ Sun] — check/uncheck in 1 click
     - Standard Start Time: e.g. 17:00
     - Standard End Time: e.g. 22:00
     - Default Price Per Hour: £[X.XX]
   • Button: 🚀 **"APPLY TO ALL SELECTED DAYS"** (bright neon purple — most visible button)
   • RESULT: Every selected day instantly becomes **FULLY AVAILABLE 17:00–22:00** — open flexible time, no fixed slots. Players can book ANY hours here.
   • Save as Default: ✅ **"SAVE THESE AS MY STANDARD HOURS"** — next time you open, these values are already filled.

---
🔹 2. FULL DAY AVAILABILITY — INSTANT OPEN TIME
✅ **"SET FULL DAY AVAILABILITY"**
   • Day dropdown → Start → End → Price → ✅ "SET FULL DAY"
   • Result: Day marked **✅ FULLY AVAILABLE [08:00–22:00] | £15/hr**
   • Players choose start time + any duration (1, 2, 3+ hours)
   • Color: Green highlight — clear visual difference from fixed slots

---
🔹 3. COPY / DUPLICATE / MULTIPLY — 1 CLICK = MULTIPLE DAYS 📋
✅ **COPY DAY TO DAYS**
   • Step 1: Pick source day (e.g. Monday already set)
   • Step 2: Select target days: [Tue] [Wed] [Thu] [Fri] — checkboxes
   • Step 3: 📋 **"COPY TO SELECTED DAYS"** — exact same times + price copied instantly

✅ **MULTIPLY SLOTS** (for when you still want fixed slots)
   • Add one slot → click ⚡ **"MULTIPLY THIS SLOT"** → choose days → done. No retyping.

✅ **COPY TO NEXT WEEK / NEXT 4 WEEKS**
   • Button: ⏭️ **"REPEAT THIS WEEK’S AVAILABILITY"**
   • Options:
     - Repeat every week (permanent until changed)
     - Repeat next 2 weeks
     - Repeat next 4 weeks
   • One click — no weekly updates needed anymore

---
🔹 4. SMART PRESETS — SAVE TIME TEMPLATES 💾
✅ **SAVE PRESET**
   • After setting hours you use often: 💾 **"SAVE AS PRESET: EVENING SHIFT / WEEKEND SHIFT / FULL DAY"**
✅ **LOAD PRESET**
   • Dropdown: "Evening Shift" → ✅ **"APPLY PRESET"** — entire week fills instantly
